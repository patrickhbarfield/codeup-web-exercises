    Earning Tokens: HowTo
  • Download Metamask crypto-wallet in Mozilla METAMASK
  • Connect your METAMASK wallet to the DAO-TEX blockchain Dapp DAO-TEX
  • Confirm wallet setup by sending some Test Ether Test your Wallet
  • Click the "Shovel Icon" and choose the enigma block you want to solve/mine
  • Once completed Click the "MineMind" button
  • DAO-TEX coin will automatically be deposited into your METAMASK wallet
