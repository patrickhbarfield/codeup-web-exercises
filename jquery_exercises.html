Get DAO-TEX

    Aquiring Coin
  1. Download Metamask crypto-wallet in Mozilla METAMASK
  2. Connect your METAMASK wallet to the DAO-TEX blockchain Dapp DAO-TEX
  3. Confirm wallet setup by sending some Test Ether Test your Wallet
  4. Click the "Shovel Icon" and choose the enigma block you want to solve/mine
  5. Once completed Click the "MineMind" button
  6. DAO-TEX coin will automatically be deposited into your METAMASK wallet
